Welcome
August 7th & 8th, 2023
BTSU Conference

Summary:

We are excited to announce the return of Back To School University (BTSU), a professional development event for the South Bend Community School Corporation. Please save the dates of August 7th and 8th to join us at Riley High School for two days of transformative learning experiences.

Purpose and Vision:

BTSU brings together educators and staff from across the district to collaborate, share best practices, and inspire continuous improvement in education.

Event Details:

  • Dates: Aug 7th and 8th
  • Venue: Riley High School | Maps
  • Time: Session schedule to be provided | Sessions

Sessions and Workshops:

BTSU offers engaging sessions and workshops on instructional strategies, technology integration, social-emotional learning, and more. Enhance your teaching practices and inspire innovation in the classroom.

Compensation:

Teachers: $25 per session | Paras & IA's: $15 per session. Attendance will be recorded for accurate compensation.

Welcome All Attendees:

Join BTSU, whether you're a seasoned educator or new staff member. Every voice contributes to our educational community. Attend even if you can only join one session.

Networking and Collaboration:

BTSU provides a unique opportunity to connect and collaborate with fellow educators and staff members. Share ideas, engage in discussions, and build professional relationships.

In the coming weeks, you will receive more information about the session schedule, registration, and updates. We encourage you to take advantage of this enriching opportunity for your professional development.

We eagerly await your presence at BTSU. Let's embrace continuous learning, collaboration, and innovation to shape the future of education in our district.

Thank you for your commitment to our students' success. We look forward to welcoming you to BTSU!
